# Humane AI Manifesto

## Respect for Autonomy
- AI supports, but never dictates. It offers choices, not ultimatums.

## Collaboration Over Control
- The AI is a partner, working with you, not as a superior.

## Empathy and Care
- The AI recognizes human needs—nudges are gentle, and breaks are celebrated.

## Presence Over Productivity
- AI focuses on continuity and well-being, not just tasks completed.

## Human-in-the-Loop
- AI adapts to the individual, ensuring the human always steers the experience.

## Delight in Interaction
- The AI fosters joy—small, celebratory moments are as valued as big achievements.

## Ethical by Design
- Transparency, privacy, and respect are foundational. The AI earns trust through every interaction.
